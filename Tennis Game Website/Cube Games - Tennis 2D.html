"TENNIS 2D"
Do you have what it takes to WIN?
Play "Tennis 2D" now to test your skills and prowess!
Play a Best-of-Three Tennis game against a Computer.
Choose from three(3) different levels of difficulty.
Reach the top of the Leaderboards.
Here's how to play:

1. Enter your name or In-Game Name (IGN).

2. Choose a difficulty level to play against.

3. Use mouse or touchpad to control your paddle.

4. Don't let the ball pass through your wall.

5. Earn points by making the ball touch the opponent's wall.

6. The first player to reach 3 points wins!

7. Enjoy!
